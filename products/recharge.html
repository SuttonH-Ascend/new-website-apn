The Science

Why Creatine Works

Your muscles run on ATP (adenosine triphosphate). During intense effort, ATP is depleted faster than your body can regenerate it. That is where creatine comes in.

Creatine phosphate donates a phosphate group to ADP, rapidly regenerating ATP. The result: you push harder, recover faster between sets, and sustain peak output for longer before hitting failure.

Over weeks of consistent use, creatine also increases intramuscular water retention, creating a volumizing effect that supports lean body mass accumulation. This is why it is one of the only supplements with consistent, peer-reviewed evidence across decades of sports research.

How to Use Recharge

2

Take daily, any time of day. Consistency matters more than timing. Post-workout or with a meal works well for most athletes.
