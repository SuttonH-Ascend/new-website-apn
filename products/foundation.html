Directions

How to Use Foundation

1
Take It

Swallow 1 veggie capsule with a meal. A single capsule delivers the full D3/K2/Calcium dose in a compact, easy-to-take form.

2
Time It

Take with a meal that contains fat. D3 and K2 are fat-soluble vitamins that absorb best in the presence of dietary fat. Taking with your largest meal is ideal.

3
Be Consistent

The value of a multivitamin is in consistent daily coverage, not acute doses. Use Foundation every day to maintain optimal micronutrient status for training and recovery.

Store in a cool, dry place. Keep out of reach of children. Consult a healthcare professional before use if you have any medical conditions.
